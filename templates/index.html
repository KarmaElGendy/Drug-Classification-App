Welcome to the Drug Classification App

This tool helps classify drugs based on patient attributes. Please enter the following details:

  • Age: Age of the patient in years.
  • Sex: Gender of the patient (M or F).
  • BP: Blood Pressure levels (LOW, NORMAL, HIGH).
  • Cholesterol: Cholesterol levels (NORMAL, HIGH).
  • Na_to_K: Sodium to Potassium ratio in the blood.
